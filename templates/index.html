our Cycles  

Orange Cat is a one-product company, renowned for its exceptionally high standards of quality control and outstanding customer support. Our exclusive catalogue features two models of the Corinto Collection, each a masterpiece of Italian luxury and exquisite craftsmanship on wheels. Crafted in Italy, these bicycles are not just modes of transportation, but symbols of a rich heritage and artistic excellence. The timeless and elegant lines of the Corinto bicycles embody the mission, vision, and values of their manufacturers, offering a ride that feels like gliding through royal courts with the sophistication and style that only Italian design can provide.

Corinto Arrow

Running collection

Corinto Arrow, with its sleek and strong design, combines a timeless feel with modern reliability. The longer and narrower saddle, and the wider distance between the padding and the top tube, ensure a dynamic riding experience for both expert and leisure riders.

Corinto Bow was designed to reflect a softer style with curves that allow a gentle mount and dismount. about
orange cat

It all started several years ago with a trip to Italy, during which time, our founder was gifted a beautifully handcrafted vintage bicycle by a close friend. Taken in by its features, he decided to bring it back to Canada and have a piece of Italy close to home. For years he rode this bicycle on the streets of downtown Toronto receiving compliments from passersby and drivers at stoplights. No one had ever seen such a unique bicycle. He rode his bicycle with his head held high, the wind in his hair and a smile on his face.

And then, someone stole his bicycle. He searched far and wide but he noted that there were no retailers in the area that had similar products available, so he returned to Italy. In 2022 he established a partnership with Taurus, a vintage bicycle manufacturer, to bring their product to Canada and Orange Cat was born.
