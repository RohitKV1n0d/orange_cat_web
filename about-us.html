    • Once upon a time a king who went in bicycle

    It is not the beginning of a fairy tale but one part of our true story . The story of Taurus : a born bicycle in Milan in 1908 in a laboratory where bicycles are handcrafted with patience and care. An obsessive attention to detail that between craftsmanship, luxury and sobriety has allowed the bicycles Taurus to be loved and appreciated by an attentive and demanding public. It was no coincidence that it was also chosen by King Vittorio Emanuele III. A king's bike that over the years has been able to respect the tradition without never betraying style but continually renewing itself with innovative ideas up to the most modern solutions and sustainable like the most current ones of electric movement.
